Multi-Sig Wallet

Bitcoin holdings secured by multi-sig technology

Cryptocurrency technology is developing very fast and we are working hard to apply the most recent developments to our service. Safety and security of your funds is our top priority. Therefore, we have embedded today’s best possible crypto fund protection solution into our platform – multi-signature technology.Our multi-sig wallets make it simply impossible to transfer funds without transaction being signed by all parties involved, meaning that even in a highly unlikely event of compromising the platform, no one could be able to remit funds from your wallets.

We don't hold customer funds in one hot wallet; instead every customer is allocated with a personal wallet, ensuring that funds belong to a particular user and nobody else. Every personal wallet is issued with 3 keys, generated and stored on completely different devices, providing increased security and advanced fraud protection. The technology is designed and customized for us by BitGo – the number one name and global trendsetter in crypto currency security.
